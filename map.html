Mind Map: Methods of Foreign Language Teaching

Grammar-Translation
Direct Method
Audio-Lingual
Focus: Reading & translating texts, learning grammar
  • Builds vocabulary and grammar knowledge
  • Focuses on accuracy
  • Limited speaking practice
  • Does not emphasize communicative skills
Focus: Speaking & listening, no translation
  • Promotes fluency
  • Encourages natural language acquisition
  • Difficult for beginners
  • May not focus enough on grammar
Focus: Listening & pronunciation through drills
  • Improves pronunciation
  • Enhances listening skills
  • Lacks creativity
  • Limited focus on grammar
Communicative Language Teaching
Task-Based Learning
Focus: Communication and fluency
  • Encourages real-world interaction
  • Engages learners
  • Grammar may be neglected
Focus: Real-world tasks, problem-solving, and fluency
  • Practical application
  • Motivates learners
  • Time-consuming preparation
